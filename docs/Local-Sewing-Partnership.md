### Email Template:

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - Custom Military Gear

Hi [Shop Name],

I'm reaching out about a potential partnership opportunity. I'm starting a military supply company (Ranger Beer Supply) and looking for a skilled sewing partner to produce custom products for Ranger School students.

Products I need made:
- Waffle bottom sleep system layers
- VS-17 IR panel modifications
- Custom patrol caps with embroidery
- Velcro waffle bottom upgrades

I'm looking for:
- Quality craftsmanship
- Competitive pricing
- Reliable turnaround times
- Ability to scale as we grow

Would you be interested in discussing a partnership? I'd love to show you samples and discuss pricing.

Best,
[Your Name]
Ranger Beer Supply
```

## PHASE 3: FOLLOW UP

### After Email:
- Wait 3-5 business days
- Follow up with phone call
- Ask to meet in person with samples

### Questions to Ask:
1. What is your typical turnaround time?
2. Do you offer bulk pricing?
3. Can you work with military-spec materials?
4. What is your minimum order quantity?
5. Do you do custom embroidery?
