# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Company:** Ranger Beer Supply  
**Mission:** Supply Ranger School students with high-quality gear and custom products  
**Business Model:** E-commerce with dropship fulfillment  
**Target Market:** Ranger School candidates, Army soldiers, military enthusiasts

---

# BUSINESS MODEL

## Revenue Stream
```
Customer Order → Website → Payment → Partner Production → Ship to Customer
```

## The Flow (Confirmed)
1. **Customer** orders custom product on ranger-beers.com
2. **Payment** processed (Stripe/Snipcart)
3. **Wholesale supplier** ships raw materials/PC to sewing partner
4. **Sewing partner** does customization (sewing, embroidery, modifications)
5. **Partner** ships finished product to customer
6. **Customer** receives tracking and product

---

# SUPPLY CHAIN

## 1. Wholesale Suppliers (Raw Materials)
- SEICO - Wholesale military/tactical
- Forest City Supply - Bulk gear
- Troops Military Supply - Ranger School specialist
- US Patriot Tactical - Large inventory

## 2. Local Sewing Partners (Custom Work)
- Receive raw products from wholesale
- Add custom modifications (velcro, embroidery, etc.)
- Ship directly to customer

## 3. Customer
- Orders through website
- Pays via Stripe/Snipcart
- Receives tracking info
- Gets quality product
