# FULFILLMENT OPTIONS

## OPTION A: Dropship (Recommended for Start)

**How it works:**
- Customer orders on your site
- Order sent to sewing partner
- Partner ships directly to customer
- No inventory needed

**Pros:**
- No upfront inventory cost
- No storage space needed
- Scale automatically

**Cons:**
- Lower margins
- Less quality control
- Partner dependency

---

## OPTION B: Inventory Stocked

**How it works:**
- Order products in bulk
- Store at your location
- Ship to customers as orders come in

**Pros:**
- Higher margins
- Quality control
- Faster shipping
- Better customer experience

**Cons:**
- Upfront cost ($500-2000+)
- Storage space needed
- Risk of unsold inventory

---

## OPTION C: Hybrid

**How it works:**
- Stock popular items (patrol caps, basic supplies)
- Dropship custom/specialty items

**Pros:**
- Balance of cost vs margins
- Control over core products
- Flexibility

**Cons:**
- More complex to manage

---

## RECOMMENDATION: Start with OPTION A (Dropship)

**Why:**
1. **Low risk** - No upfront inventory
2. **Test demand** - See what sells before investing
3. **Scalable** - Grows with your business
4. **Simple** - Less logistics to manage

**When to switch to inventory:**
- When you have consistent sales data
- When margins become too thin
- When you want faster shipping

## DROPSHIP WORKFLOW (CONFIRMED)

```
1. Customer orders on ranger-beers.com
   ↓
2. Payment processed (Stripe/Snipcart)
   ↓
3. You receive notification
   ↓
4. Order sent to wholesale supplier (raw materials)
   ↓
5. Wholesale ships components to SEWING PARTNER
   ↓
6. Sewing partner receives PC (product components)
   ↓
7. Partner does sewing/customization
   ↓
8. Partner ships finished product to CUSTOMER
   ↓
9. Tracking sent to customer
```

### Example: Custom Waffle Bottoms
1. Customer orders "Waffle Bottoms w/ Velcro" - $59.99
2. Payment received
3. You notify wholesale: "Ship 1x waffle bottom to [Partner address]"
4. Wholesale ships raw waffle bottom to partner
5. Partner adds velcro, does quality check
6. Partner ships to customer
