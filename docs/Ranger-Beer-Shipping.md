# SHIPPING OPTIONS

## OPTION A: You Handle Shipping (Recommended for Start)

**How it works:**
1. Sewing partner finishes product
2. Partner notifies you
3. You (or local pickup) get item from partner
4. You ship to customer via USPS/Amazon

**Pros:**
- Partners don't need shipping experience
- You control quality before shipping
- Simpler for partners

**Cons:**
- Your time for pickups
- Need transportation

**Estimated Time:**
- Partner: 1-2 days to make item
- You: Pick up 1x/week or per order
- Ship: 2-3 days to customer

---

## OPTION B: Partner Ships Direct

**How it works:**
1. Sewing partner finishes product
2. Partner packs and ships directly to customer
3. Partner provides tracking #

**Pros:**
- Zero work for you
- Faster shipping possible

**Cons:**
- Partners may not have shipping accounts
- Quality control harder
- Partners may charge more

**Requirements for Partners:**
- PayPal or shipping account
- Basic packing supplies
- Willingness to ship

---

## OPTION C: Hybrid

**How it works:**
- Local partners: You pick up
- Out-of-town partners: They ship direct
- Base runners: Pick up & deliver locally

**Best of both worlds**

---

## OPTION D: Base Runners (NEW - Local Delivery)

**How it works:**
1. Sewing partner finishes product
2. Notify you / runner picks up
3. Runner delivers to customer (on base or local)
4. Customer receives same-day/next-day

**Pros:**
- Faster than shipping
- No shipping costs
- Works great for base locals
- Extra income for runners

**Who are runners?**
- People living on/near base
- Soldiers with vehicles
- Looking for side income

**Payment:**
- Pay runners per delivery ($5-10)
- Or offer free products in exchange

---

## HOW RUNNER SYSTEM WORKS

```
ORDER PLACED
     ↓
SEWING PARTNER COMPLETES ITEM
     ↓
NOTIFY RUNNER (via app/phone)
     ↓
RUNNER PICKS UP FROM PARTNER
     ↓
RUNNER DELIVERS TO CUSTOMER (same day)
     ↓
CUSTOMER CONFIRMS RECEIPT
     ↓
RUNNER GETS PAID
```

## RECOMMENDATION: START WITH OPTION A

**Why:**
- Partners don't need shipping setup
- You control quality
- Simpler for everyone
- Test the model first

**When to switch to Option B:**
- Partner shows they can ship reliably
- You want to scale and reduce pickups
- Partner requests it
