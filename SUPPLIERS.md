# Ranger Beers — Supplier Strategy

## Tier 1: Custom Manufacturing (highest margin, longest lead time)

### Custom Patrol Cap (VS-17 + Cat Eyes)
- Contact domestic mil-spec sewers (e.g., Propper, TRU-SPEC custom programs, or Alibaba for bulk)
- VS-17 panel fabric available from military surplus
- Cat eye reflective tape from 3M
- **Lead time:** 8-12 weeks for first run
- **MOQ:** TBD — get quotes from 2-3 manufacturers

### Waffle Bottoms w/ Velcro Legs
- Contact ECWCS layer manufacturers
- Velcro modification is a custom sew job — find a domestic cut-and-sew shop or a mil-spec apparel manufacturer
- **Lead time:** 6-10 weeks
- **MOQ:** TBD — likely 50-100 minimum

## Action Items
