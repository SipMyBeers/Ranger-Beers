Ranger Creed Mastery

Land Navigation Course
Master terrain association, map reading, compass work, and point plotting. Day and night land nav are RAP Week gates — this course covers everything from basic map skills to advanced resection techniques.
8 modules covering every land navigation skill tested at Ranger School. Starts with map reading fundamentals (1:50,000 scale, 5 map colors, marginal information, declination diagrams) and builds to 8-digit grid coordinate plotting with a military protractor. Covers lensatic compass techniques (centerhold and compass-to-cheek), azimuth shooting, back azimuth calculation, and G-M angle conversion. Terrain association module teaches the 10 terrain features and 4 navigation techniques (catching features, handrails, attack points, backstops). Dedicated modules for day land nav strategy (4 of 6 points in 5 hours) and night land nav strategy (pace count, handrails, bezel ring navigation). Closes with 6 practice scenarios including a full timed simulation.
