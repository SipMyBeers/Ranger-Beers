Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a subscription?

No. All plans are one-time purchases. You pay once and keep access forever.

What's included in a school pass?

A school pass unlocks every course within a single school. For example, the Ranger School pass gives you access to all 19 Ranger courses — land nav, knots, battle drills, PT programs, and more.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Module 1 of every course is free to preview. Just sign in and start learning.

Do plans include gear?

Plans include access to courses and training content. Gear is purchased separately through each school's shop.

What if new courses are added?

All Access members get every new course automatically. School pass holders get new courses added to their school. Individual course purchases cover that course only.
