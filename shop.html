Build the Brief That Gets You a GO

Every graded patrol requires a terrain model for your OPORD brief. You need a macro model (big picture — route to objective) and a micro model (zoomed in — actions on the OBJ). Build them right and the RI sees a leader who knows their plan.

Macro Terrain Model

Shows the big picture — current position, route, phase lines, checkpoints, and ORP location. Built first during WARNO development. Used to brief Paragraph 1 (Situation) and movement plan.

Micro / Objective Terrain Model
