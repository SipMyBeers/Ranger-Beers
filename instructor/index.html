Course Overview

What is the Army Instructor Badge?

The Army Instructor Badge is an identification badge awarded to Soldiers who serve as instructors at institutional Army training venues under the U.S. Army Training and Doctrine Command (TRADOC). The badge recognizes the critical contribution that quality instruction makes to Army readiness.

It comes in three degrees: Basic Instructor (awarded upon completion of instructor certification and one year of service), Senior Instructor (requires completion of the Faculty Development Program Phase II and three years of instructor service), and Master Instructor (requires completion of TRADOC's Master Instructor Program, demonstrating exceptional teaching ability, instructional design skills, and mentorship). The Master Instructor Badge is relatively rare and signifies the highest level of teaching proficiency in the Army.

Course Phases

Three Degrees of Excellence

  • 2-4 Wks Instructor Certification — Army Basic Instructor Course (ABIC), small group instructor training, or equivalent certification. Covers instructional methods, lesson plan development, and classroom management.
  • Progressive Faculty Development Program — Phase I (new instructor orientation), Phase II (advanced facilitation and evaluation techniques, required for Senior badge), Phase III (curriculum development and program management).
  • Selection Master Instructor Program — TRADOC Master Instructor evaluation board, portfolio review, teaching demonstration, peer evaluation, and demonstrated excellence over 4+ years of instructor service.
Key Standards

What It Takes

Basic: Complete instructor certification + 1 year instructor duty. Senior: Complete Faculty Development Phase II + 3 years instructor duty with documented excellence. Master: Complete Master Instructor Program evaluation + 4+ years with exceptional teaching record, published instructional materials, and mentorship of other instructors.

All three degrees require an active instructor assignment at a TRADOC school or accredited training institution.
