Overview

What You'll Do

As a Veterinary Food Inspection Specialist (68R), you perform food safety inspections and quality control procedures in Army facilities. You'll ensure that all food supplies meet the highest standards of safety and quality, providing critical public health support in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform food safety and quality control inspections
  • Maintain and troubleshoot food inspection equipment
  • Execute public health missions to support unit readiness
  • Coordinate with local and joint health agencies
  • Ensure compliance with food safety and security standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sam Houston, Texas. Specialized training in biology, chemistry, and public health doctrine.
