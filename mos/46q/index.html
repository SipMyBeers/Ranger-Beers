Overview

What You'll Do

As a Public Affairs Specialist (46Q), you tell the Army's story through diverse media. You'll write news articles, capture mission photography, and manage community relations to ensure that the force and the public are informed about Army operations in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Write news articles and press releases for Army publications
  • Capture mission-critical photography of Soldiers and operations
  • Manage community relations and media interactions
  • Execute information operations to support unit objectives
  • Ensure compliance with public affairs and media standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort George G. Meade, Maryland. Specialized training in journalism, photography, and public relations.
