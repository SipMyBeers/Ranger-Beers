Overview

What You'll Do

As an Internment/Resettlement Specialist (31E), you manage the custody, control, and welfare of detainees and prisoners. You'll ensure the security of confinement facilities, provide humanitarian support in resettlement operations, and maintain the highest standards of safety and discipline.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage the custody and control of detainees and prisoners
  • Operate and maintain internment and confinement facilities
  • Provide humanitarian support and oversight in resettlement operations
  • Ensure compliance with international law and safety standards
  • Perform tactical movement and security of detainees

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri. Specialized training in confinement procedures, humanitarian law, and tactical security.
