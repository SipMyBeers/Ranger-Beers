Overview

What You'll Do

As a Satellite/Microwave Systems Chief (25T), you oversee the maintenance and operation of advanced satellite and microwave platforms. You'll lead teams of specialized operators to establish and maintain global data links, ensuring fleet readiness.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supervise the operation and maintenance of satellite and microwave systems
  • Interpret technical manuals and maintenance specifications
  • Manage maintenance materials, tools, and personnel
  • Ensure maintenance quality control and safety standards
  • Provide technical guidance to subordinate operator teams

Career Phases

PHASE 1
AIT
Duration18 Weeks
LocationFort Eisenhower, GA

Complete Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training. Master the fundamental technical and tactical skills required for 25T operations.

PHASE 2
First Units

Arrival at first duty station. Focus on mastering individual and team tasks, attaining platform certifications, and integrating into operational missions.

PHASE 3
Team Leadership

Promotion to Sergeant and Staff Sergeant. Leading teams and squads, managing equipment readiness, and attending specialized leadership schools.

PHASE 4
Senior Enlisted

Platoon Sergeant and First Sergeant roles. Managing large-scale operations, mentorship of junior NCOs, and advising senior commanders.
