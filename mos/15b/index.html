Overview

What You'll Do

As an Aviation Combined Arms Operations Officer (15B), you synchronize aviation assets with ground maneuver forces. You'll plan complex multi-domain operations, ensuring that air power is applied effectively to support tactical and operational objectives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan and synchronize aviation operations with ground units
  • Direct the employment of diverse aviation assets in combined arms missions
  • Develop tactical air maneuver plans and mission orders
  • Coordinate with joint and multinational partners for air support
  • Advise senior commanders on aviation capabilities and limitations

Training

Aviation Officer professional development through years of flight experience followed by Advanced Operations courses at Fort Rucker.
