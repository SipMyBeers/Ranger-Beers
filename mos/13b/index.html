Cannon Crewmember

The muscle behind the King of Battle. Cannon Crewmembers operate the Army's powerful howitzers to deliver devastating fire support.

Overview

What You'll Do

As a Cannon Crewmember (13B), you operate and maintain howitzer weapon systems. You'll work in teams to load, aim, and fire artillery pieces, ensuring that indirect fire is delivered with precision and speed to support ground forces.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain howitzer weapon systems (M119, M777, M109)
  • Load and fire artillery rounds according to fire mission data
  • Execute gunnery and tactical operations in all environments
  • Perform maintenance on artillery pieces and support vehicles
  • Operate digital communication and navigation equipment

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. Training focuses on howitzer operation, gunnery, and field artillery tactics.
