Overview

What You'll Do

As an Avenger Crewmember (14S), you operate and maintain the Avenger air defense system. You'll destroy enemy aircraft and missiles using vehicle-mounted Stinger missiles and machine guns, providing mobile air defense for ground forces.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain Avenger weapon systems and vehicles
  • Detect, track, and identify short-range aerial threats
  • Execute missile and machine gun engagements against aerial targets
  • Maintain and troubleshoot weapon platform electronics
  • Perform tactical movement and reconnaissance for mobile air defense

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. Training focuses on mobile air defense tactics, weapons operation, and target identification.
