Overview

What You'll Do

Armor Enlistment Option (19U) is the designated MOS for recruits who have not yet been assigned to a specific armor role. During training, you will be assigned as either an 19K (Abrams Tanker) or 19D (Cavalry Scout) based on the needs of the Army.

Key Responsibilities

  • Complete One Station Unit Training (OSUT)
  • Master basic armor skills and combat operations
  • Learn tactical movement and weapon system maintenance
  • Prepare for assignment to an Armor or Cavalry unit
  • Earn the designation of an Armor Soldier

Training

OSUT (One Station Unit Training) at Fort Benning, Georgia, combining Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training into one continuous course.
