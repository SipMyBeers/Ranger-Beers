Overview

What You'll Do

As a Fire Direction Specialist (13E), you operate the fire direction systems for cannon artillery. You'll calculate ballistic data, manage firing missions, and ensure the precise delivery of indirect fire support to ground forces.

Key Responsibilities

  • Calculate firing data for cannon artillery missions
  • Operate fire direction computers and communication equipment
  • Manage fire mission execution and coordination
  • Monitor ballistic weather data and mission parameters
  • Maintain and troubleshoot fire direction systems

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. Specialized training in fire direction doctrine, gunnery, and computer operation.
