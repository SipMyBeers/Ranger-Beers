Overview

What You'll Do

As a Technical Engineer (12T), you provide the critical data needed for engineering projects. You'll conduct land surveys, create technical drawings, and perform soil and material analysis to ensure project success.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct land and hydrographic surveys
  • Create technical drawings and blueprints using CAD
  • Perform soil analysis and material testing
  • Manage engineering data and project documentation
  • Ensure project compliance with engineering standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri. Specialized training in surveying, drafting, and material analysis.
