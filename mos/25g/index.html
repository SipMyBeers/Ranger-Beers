Overview

What You'll Do

As a Mobile Subscriber Equipment (MSE) Operator (25G), you operate and maintain mobile communication platforms. You'll ensure that voice and data transmission is available to tactical units, providing the mobility and connectivity needed for maneuver operations in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain mobile communication equipment and vehicles
  • Ensure voice and data connectivity for tactical units on the move
  • Troubleshoot MSE platform failures and malfunctions
  • Perform tactical movement and site selection for communication operations
  • Ensure compliance with communication safety and security standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia. Specialized training in radio physics, mobile networking, and communication tactics.
