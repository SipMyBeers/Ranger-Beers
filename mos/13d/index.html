Overview

What You'll Do

As a Tactical Data System Specialist (13D), you operate the Advanced Field Artillery Tactical Data System (AFATDS). You'll process fire mission data, calculate ballistic solutions, and manage digital communication between observers and firing batteries.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain AFATDS and other tactical computers
  • Process fire mission requests and calculate firing data
  • Maintain digital communication networks for fire support
  • Ensure accuracy of ballistic calculations and target data
  • Coordinate fire missions between multiple units

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. Specialized training in computer operation, gunnery, and fire direction.
