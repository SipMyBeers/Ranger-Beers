Overview

What You'll Do

As an Electromagnetic Spectrum Manager (25E), you coordinate and manage the Army's use of the electromagnetic spectrum. You'll ensure that communication systems, radars, and other electronic devices have the frequencies they need to operate without interference in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Coordinate and manage electromagnetic spectrum use for tactical units
  • Assign and monitor radio frequencies to ensure interference-free communication
  • Perform electromagnetic interference analysis and resolution
  • Manage spectrum management databases and software
  • Ensure compliance with spectrum management standards and regulations

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia. Specialized training in spectrum management, radio physics, and electronic warfare.
