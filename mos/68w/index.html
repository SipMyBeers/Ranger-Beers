Overview

What You'll Do

As a Combat Medic Specialist (68W), you provide emergency medical treatment and primary healthcare to Soldiers. You'll execute tactical combat casualty care on the front lines and provide critical support to medical teams.

Career Phases

PHASE 1
Medical AIT
AIT16 Weeks
LocationFort Sam Houston, TX

AIT at the Medical Education and Training Campus (METC). Training includes EMT-Basic certification, trauma management, and tactical combat casualty care (TCCC).

PHASE 2
First Units

Arrival at unit (Line Medic or Aid Station). Mastering sick call procedures, field sanitation, and individual medical readiness. Attending specialized schools like EFMB.

PHASE 3
Medical Leader

Sergeant and Staff Sergeant roles. Managing medical squads, providing oversight for junior medics, and ensuring medical support for unit operations.

PHASE 4
Senior Enlisted

Platoon Sergeant or First Sergeant. Managing large-scale medical operations, logistics, and personnel readiness across the formation.
