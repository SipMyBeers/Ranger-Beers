Overview

What You'll Do

As a Military Government Specialist (38G), you execute governance and restoration missions in all environments. You'll coordinate with local governments, identify infrastructure needs, and support the restoration of essential services to support tactical and operational objectives across the battlefield.

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute governance and restoration missions in support of local governments
  • Perform assessment of civilian infrastructure and governance needs
  • Coordinate with local governments and international organizations
  • Train indigenous forces in governance and infrastructure restoration
  • Manage unit resources and logistics in austere environments

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Bragg, North Carolina. Specialized training in governance doctrine, cultural analysis, and infrastructure restoration.
