Overview

What You'll Do

As a Signals Intelligence Senior Sergeant (35V), you have reached the pinnacle of NCO leadership in the SIGINT branch. You serve as the senior enlisted advisor to commanders, ensuring operational readiness and expert execution of all spectrum intelligence missions across the battlefield.

Key Responsibilities

  • Advise commanders on all matters involving enlisted SIGINT Soldiers
  • Oversee the training and professional development of SIGINT NCOs
  • Manage large-scale intelligence operations and logistics
  • Ensure the highest standards of discipline and performance
  • Serve as a subject matter expert on signals intelligence doctrine

Training

Attainment of senior NCO rank through years of distinguished service in SIGINT MOSs. Completion of the Sergeants Major Academy and other senior leadership courses.

Ready for Command?
