Overview

What You'll Do

As a Human Intelligence Collector (35M), you collect and analyze information from human sources. You'll conduct interrogations, debriefings, and source operations to identify enemy units, intentions, and capabilities, providing critical intelligence support to commanders in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct interrogations and debriefings of human sources
  • Execute source operations to collect intelligence from individuals
  • Translate and interpret foreign languages for intelligence purposes
  • Coordinate with intelligence and joint partners for support
  • Maintain and troubleshoot advanced digital and audio equipment

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Huachuca, Arizona and Defense Language Institute (if applicable). Specialized training in interrogation, source operations, and language.
