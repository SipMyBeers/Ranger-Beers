Overview

What You'll Do

As a Visual Information Operations Chief (25Z), you oversee the maintenance and operation of advanced visual information platforms. You'll lead teams of specialized producers to capture and distribute imagery, ensuring fleet readiness.

Key Responsibilities

  • Supervise the capture and production of visual information
  • Interpret technical manuals and production specifications
  • Manage production materials, tools, and personnel
  • Ensure production quality control and safety standards
  • Provide technical guidance to subordinate producer teams

Training

NCO professional development through years of experience in visual information MOSs (25V, 25R, etc.) followed by Advanced NCO courses at Fort George G. Meade.
