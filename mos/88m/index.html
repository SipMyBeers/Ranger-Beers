Overview

What You'll Do

As a Motor Transport Operator (88M), you operate and maintain diverse Army vehicles, from Humvees to heavy tactical trucks. You'll ensure the safe transport of personnel and cargo, providing critical logistical support to tactical units in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain diverse tactical and commercial vehicles
  • Ensure the safe transport of personnel and cargo in all environments
  • Execute logistical and convoy operations missions
  • Troubleshoot vehicle platform failures and malfunctions
  • Ensure compliance with transportation safety and security standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri. Specialized training in vehicle operation, mechanics, and transportation doctrine.
