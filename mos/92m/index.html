Overview

What You'll Do

As a Mortuary Affairs Specialist (92M), you manage the recovery, identification, and processing of remains. You'll ensure that all personal effects are inventoried and that the fallen are prepared for transport with the highest standards of care and dignity in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform the search, recovery, and identification of remains
  • Execute mortuary operations and personal effects processing
  • Ensure the highest standards of dignity and respect for the fallen
  • Manage unit records and documentation for mortuary affairs
  • Ensure compliance with mortuary safety and legal standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Lee, Virginia. Specialized training in anatomy, forensic analysis, and mortuary doctrine.
