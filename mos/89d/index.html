Overview

What You'll Do

As an EOD Specialist (89D), you execute detection and disposal missions in all environments. You'll use advanced equipment to identify hazards, perform mass decontamination, and support tactical objectives while protecting the force from explosive threats.

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute specialized detection and disposal missions for diverse ordnance
  • Perform mass and equipment decontamination operations
  • Operate advanced hazard detection and protection systems
  • Coordinate with medical and intelligence partners for support
  • Maintain and troubleshoot advanced EOD equipment

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Phase 1 and Phase 2 of the EOD Course at Fort Lee, Virginia and Eglin Air Force Base, Florida. Intense technical training in chemistry, biology, physics, and disposal tactics.
