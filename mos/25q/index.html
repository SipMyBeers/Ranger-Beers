Overview

What You'll Do

As a Multichannel Transmission Systems Operator-Maintainer (25Q), you operate and maintain the Army's tactical transmission platforms. You'll ensure that multichannel voice and data connectivity is available to tactical units, providing the bandwidth needed for maneuver operations in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain multichannel transmission systems and vehicles
  • Ensure voice and data connectivity for tactical units
  • Troubleshoot transmission platform failures and malfunctions
  • Perform tactical movement and site selection for transmission operations
  • Ensure compliance with communication safety and security standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia. Specialized training in radio physics, multichannel networking, and communication tactics.
