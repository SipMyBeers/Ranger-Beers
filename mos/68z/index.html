Overview

What You'll Do

As a Chief Medical NCO (68Z), you have reached the pinnacle of NCO leadership in the medical branch. You serve as the senior enlisted advisor to commanders, ensuring operational readiness and expert execution of all medical and clinical missions across the force.

Key Responsibilities

  • Advise commanders on all matters involving enlisted medical Soldiers
  • Oversee the training and professional development of medical NCOs
  • Manage large-scale medical operations and logistics
  • Ensure the highest standards of discipline and performance
  • Serve as a subject matter expert on medical doctrine

Career Phases

PHASE 1
AIT
Duration20 Weeks
LocationFort Sam Houston, TX

Complete Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training. Master the fundamental technical and tactical skills required for 68Z operations.

PHASE 2
First Units

Arrival at first duty station. Focus on mastering individual and team tasks, attaining platform certifications, and integrating into operational missions.

PHASE 3
Team Leadership

Promotion to Sergeant and Staff Sergeant. Leading teams and squads, managing equipment readiness, and attending specialized leadership schools.

PHASE 4
Senior Enlisted

Platoon Sergeant and First Sergeant roles. Managing large-scale operations, mentorship of junior NCOs, and advising senior commanders.
