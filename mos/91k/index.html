Overview

What You'll Do

As an Armament Repairer (91K), you maintain and repair the diverse range of weapon systems in Army units. You'll ensure that the rifles, machine guns, and anti-tank systems are in top condition, providing critical lethality support in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and repair armament platforms and weapon systems
  • Inspect and service optical guidance and targeting equipment
  • Troubleshoot weapon platform failures and malfunctions
  • Use specialized tools and diagnostic equipment
  • Ensure compliance with ordnance safety and maintenance standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Lee, Virginia. Specialized training in mechanics, electronics, and weapon system operation.
