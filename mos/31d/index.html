Overview

What You'll Do

As a CID Special Agent (31D), you conduct complex criminal investigations involving Army personnel and property. You'll use advanced forensic tools and investigative techniques to solve crimes, providing critical support to the military justice system.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct complex criminal investigations and forensic analysis
  • Perform interviews and interrogations of suspects and witnesses
  • Use advanced digital and physical forensic tools
  • Coordinate with local and joint law enforcement agencies
  • Ensure compliance with legal and investigative standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri. Specialized training in forensic analysis, criminal law, and investigative techniques.
