Overview

What You'll Do

As an Aircraft Pneudraulics Repairer (15H), you maintain and repair the hydraulic and pneumatic systems of Army aircraft. You'll ensure that the landing gear, flight controls, and other pressurized systems are in top condition, ensuring the safety and performance of the fleet.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and repair aircraft hydraulic and pneumatic systems
  • Inspect and service landing gear and flight control actuators
  • Troubleshoot pneudraulic system failures and malfunctions
  • Use specialized tools and diagnostic equipment
  • Ensure compliance with aviation safety and maintenance standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Joint Base Eustis-Langley, Virginia. Specialized training in hydraulics, pneumatics, and aircraft mechanics.
