Overview

What You'll Do

As a Military Intelligence Systems Maintainer/Integrator (35T), you install, maintain, and repair the advanced electronic systems in MI units. You'll ensure that the data links, servers, and collection systems are reliable and secure, providing critical technical support in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and repair MI digital networks and servers
  • Inspect and service intelligence collection platforms
  • Troubleshoot intelligence system failures and technical malfunctions
  • Use specialized tools and diagnostic equipment
  • Ensure compliance with cyber safety and security standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Huachuca, Arizona. Specialized training in electronics, networking, and intelligence mechanics.
