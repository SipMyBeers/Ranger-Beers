Overview

What You'll Do

As a Cyber Network Defender (17D), you maintain and protect the Army's digital networks. You'll ensure that the network is resilient and secure, detecting and neutralizing cyber threats to ensure mission success in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and protect aircraft avionics and navigation systems
  • Maintain and protect Army digital networks and systems
  • Detect, track, and neutralize digital threats and vulnerabilities
  • Interpret electronic schematics and technical manuals
  • Ensure compliance with cyber safety and maintenance standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia. Specialized training in networking, security, and cyber defense tactics.
