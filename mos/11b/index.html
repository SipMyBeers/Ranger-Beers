Overview

What You'll Do

As an Infantryman (11B), you'll be the Army's primary ground combat force. You'll engage in close-range combat, operate machine guns, and execute dismounted patrol operations in all types of terrain.

Career Phases

PHASE 1
Infantry OSUT
Duration22 Weeks
LocationFort Moore, GA

Infantry One Station Unit Training (OSUT) combines Basic Combat Training and Advanced Individual Training into one continuous course. You'll master rifle marksmanship, land navigation, and individual tactical skills.

PHASE 2
Line Company

Arrival at your first duty station (82nd, 101st, 10th Mountain, etc.). Focus on mastering squad-level tactics, weapon systems (M249, M240L), and attending specialized schools like Airborne or Air Assault.

PHASE 3
NCO Corps

Promotion to Sergeant (E-5) and Staff Sergeant (E-6). Leading a 4-man fire team or 9-man squad. Attending Ranger School and Advanced Leader Course (ALC).

PHASE 4
Senior Enlisted

Sergeant First Class (E-7) and above. Roles include Platoon Sergeant, First Sergeant, and Command Sergeant Major. Managing large-scale operations and mentorship.
