Overview

What You'll Do

As a Military Working Dog Handler (31K), you partner with a canine to execute specialized security and detection missions. You'll ensure the safety of personnel and facilities by detecting explosives and narcotics, and providing tactical support in reconnaissance and patrol operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute specialized detection missions with a military working dog
  • Ensure the health, welfare, and training of the MWD
  • Perform security patrols and obstacle clearance
  • Coordinate with local and joint law enforcement agencies
  • Ensure compliance with MWD safety and handling standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Lackland Air Force Base, Texas. Specialized training in canine handling, behavior, and tactical operations.
