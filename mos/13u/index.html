Overview

What You'll Do

As a Field Artillery Sensor Specialist (13U), you operate and maintain advanced sensor systems used for target acquisition. You'll ensure that the artillery has the best data possible to engage targets with precision and speed.

MOS Training Pipeline

Basic Combat Training (BCT)
Phase 1-3
Duration10 Weeks
FocusWarrior Tasks
RequirementACFT Pass

The transformation from civilian to Soldier. You will master marksmanship, land navigation, and basic combat drills. This phase builds the discipline and physical foundation required for all Field Artillery Soldiers.

Advanced Individual Training (AIT)
Technical Phase
LocationFort Sill, OK

Master the fundamental technical and tactical skills required for 13U operations. Training focuses on system operations, maintenance, and branch-specific doctrine.

Tactical Validation
Field Phase
CulminationFTX
StandardsGo/No-Go

Validate your skills in a field environment. This phase culminates in a multi-day FTX where you will execute 13U tasks under tactical conditions.

MOS Qualification & Arrival
Operational
StatusMOS Qualified

Upon successful completion of training, you are awarded the 13U MOS and report to your first operational unit. You are now a fully qualified Field Artillery Sensor Specialist.
