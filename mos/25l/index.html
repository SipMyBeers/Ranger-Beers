Overview

What You'll Do

As a Cable Systems Installer-Maintainer (25L), you install, maintain, and repair copper and fiber optic cable systems. You'll ensure that the physical layer of the network is resilient and secure, providing critical connectivity for tactical and strategic units.

Key Responsibilities

  • Install and repair copper and fiber optic cable systems
  • Maintain and troubleshoot cable distribution networks
  • Interpret cable diagrams and technical manuals
  • Test cable systems for proper operation and security
  • Ensure compliance with cable safety and maintenance standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Gordon, Georgia. Specialized training in cable installation, splicing, and network testing.
