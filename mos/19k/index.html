Overview

What You'll Do

As an M1 Armor Crewmember (19K), you operate and maintain the M1 Abrams Main Battle Tank. You'll work in four-Soldier crews to load, aim, and fire the tank's main gun and machine guns, ensuring the lethality and survivability of the heavy force in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operate and maintain M1 Abrams tank platforms
  • Load and fire the main gun and machine guns
  • Execute tactical movement and gunnery operations
  • Perform maintenance on the tank and its diverse systems
  • Operate digital communication and navigation equipment

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Benning, Georgia. Training focuses on tank operation, gunnery, and armor tactics.
