Overview

What You'll Do

As an Observation/Scout Helicopter Repairer (15V), you maintain and repair the Army's scout platforms. You'll ensure that the aircraft's airframes, engines, and flight controls are in top condition, ensuring the mobility and readiness of the fleet.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and repair scout helicopter airframes and engines
  • Inspect and service flight controls and navigation systems
  • Troubleshoot aircraft system failures and malfunctions
  • Use specialized tools and diagnostic equipment
  • Ensure compliance with aviation safety and maintenance standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Joint Base Eustis-Langley, Virginia. Specialized training in aircraft mechanics and scout-specific systems.
