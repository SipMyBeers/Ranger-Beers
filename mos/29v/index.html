Overview

What You'll Do

As an Electronic Warfare Senior Sergeant (29V), you have reached the pinnacle of NCO leadership in the Electronic Warfare branch. You serve as the senior enlisted advisor to commanders, ensuring operational readiness and expert execution of all electromagnetic warfare missions across the battlefield.

Key Responsibilities

  • Advise commanders on all matters involving enlisted EW Soldiers
  • Oversee the training and professional development of EW NCOs
  • Manage large-scale electronic warfare operations and logistics
  • Ensure the highest standards of discipline and performance
  • Serve as a subject matter expert on electromagnetic warfare doctrine

Training

Attainment of senior NCO rank through years of distinguished service in EW MOSs. Completion of the Sergeants Major Academy and other senior leadership courses.

Ready for Command?

Leading from the front requires the best gear and constant training.
