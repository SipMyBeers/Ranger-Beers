Overview

What You'll Do

As a Financial Management Officer (36A), you lead Soldiers in finance units. You'll be responsible for the planning and execution of budgeting, accounting, and resource management operations, ensuring that the force has the financial support it needs in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead Financial Management platoons and detachments in diverse missions
  • Plan and coordinate budgeting and accounting operations
  • Direct the management of unit funds and financial resources
  • Coordinate with local and joint financial institutions
  • Manage unit resources, logistics, and personnel

Training

Financial Management Officer Basic Leadership Course (FM BOLC) at Fort Jackson, South Carolina. Training focuses on leadership, accounting, and financial doctrine.
