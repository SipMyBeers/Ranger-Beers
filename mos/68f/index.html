Overview

What You'll Do

As a Physical Therapy Specialist (68F), you assist in the execution of rehabilitation programs in Army medical facilities. You'll administer physical treatments, perform patient assessments, and provide critical support to medical teams in all environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide physical therapy treatments and rehabilitation support
  • Assist physical therapists in patient evaluations and assessments
  • Execute therapeutic exercise programs for Soldiers
  • Manage rehabilitation records and patient documentation
  • Ensure compliance with physical therapy safety and treatment standards

Training

Basic Combat Training followed by Advanced Individual Training at Fort Sam Houston, Texas. Specialized training in anatomy, therapeutic techniques, and medical mechanics.
